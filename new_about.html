History

Our co-founders, Pravinchandra Mehta and Usha Mehta have been in the industry for over 50 years. We started business in 1971, under the name of Ravi Corporation. Our founders aimed to build a name in the industry, starting with door to door sales. With their zeal, hard work, and dedication, Ravi Corporation soon became a trusted name in the industry by gaining our customers' confidence with our unmatched quality and competitive rates. As the company progressed, we grew Ravi Corporation to its 2 sister companies - Ravi Packagings and Ravi Packers, where the former is our trading unit, and the latter being our manufacturing unit. We value people and therefore have a trusted team of skilled workers, many of whom have been with us since the inception of the company.


Vision

We envision ourselves as the one stop destination for all packaging needs. We aim at expanding our product line by introducing innovative products and ensuring our customers get the best possible solutions to help them deliver happiness, flawlessly.

Mission

The customer always comes first and we are on a mission to fulfill all their requirements. We prioritize client satisfaction and aim to build a brand which produces quality products at affordable rates. We promote a culture of growth and learning in our day to day working of the company and ensure our staff is given the best resources possible.


Values


Integrity

The value of integrity is core to Ravi Packagings. We work with integrity in everything we do, and embody our principles when working with customers, suppliers and colleagues. We believe in the maxim that what goes around comes around and therefore it is vital we work with trust, transparency and honesty.

Respect

Respect is a guiding value for all employees at Ravi Packagings. We believe that respect is at the core of everything, not only at work but also at home. Collaboration and productivity together create a positive workplace with satisfied employees.

Customer Delight

Customer Delight goes beyond satisfaction and loyalty. Delighted customers are created by going above and beyond the expectations and making them feel special. We continuously strive to build our Ravi Packagings family and aim at achieving maximum customer satisfaction.

Dedication

We understand the dynamic nature of the industry and push our limits till it is time to stretch again. Beneath the unruffled exterior, we work with the similar dedication that makes us a strong based company.

Excellence

We strive for continuous improvement. We produce the best products and deliver the best services as we are capable of providing. We will challenge the process, the product, and our opinions. We foster a team spirit, never challenging the person, but challenging the problem or opportunity.
