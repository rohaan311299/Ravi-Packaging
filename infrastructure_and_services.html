Film Extruder Machine


  1. Applied to blowing the plastic films like low density polythene (LDPE), high density polythene(HDPE) and linear low density polythene (LLDPE).
  2. Its specially-designed screw is of sound quality in plasticizing, which helps increase the production capacity.
  3. Multiple protection properties including puncture and heat resistant, less or zero attraction to moisture and oxygen, etc.
  4. Suitable for producing high barrier wrapping and stuffing material for packing food products, processed fluid consistency food items, etc.


Cutting Sealing Machine


  1. This bottom sealing and cutting machine is suitable for making bags without printing. It could produce two lines of bags once.
  2. It has an automatic temperature controller, static electricity remover, counter and alarm units. It's suitable for making rubbish bags or fresh-keeping bags.
  3. If replaced its dot blade with flat blade, rewind coil with flat table, this machine could make flat-rim bags and T-shirt bags.




Flexographic Printing Machine


  1. Flexographic printing system is used for transfer of image through photopolymer stereo of 2.8 mm and 4.6 mm thickness.
  2. Controlled ink transfer is a key factor in high quality flexo printing.
  3. Prints through photo polymer stereos using water or solvent based inks.
  4. Economical for small lot printing.
