Who We Are


Ravi Packagings & Ravi Packers

Ravi Packagings and Ravi Packers are sister concerns, where Ravi Packagings deals in all trading activities, whereas Ravi Packers is our manufacturing unit. We are manufacturers and traders of packaging material used in numerous industries like chemicals, fisheries, airlines, etc. Our manufacturing facility is located in Vasai, the hub of various industrial units. Having been in the market for over 50 years, we are highly experienced in the industry. Our team of trusted employees have guided and supported us along this wonderful journey. Ravi Packagings has a reputation of being positively driven and aims at satisfying all our customers' needs. Our clientele list comprises of companies which are highly respected and reputed in their respective industries. Our focus has and always will be on manufacturing quality products and providing timely delivery to our customers. While working with our clients, we ensure that we operate sustainably to provide a brighter future for the next generation. Our management is cautious about the environment and ensures we minimise our burden on the environment. Our experienced team is confidently inspired to make our companies one-stop-shop for all packaging needs.

Sustainability

We buy back used plastics and forward it to the concerned recycling centers to be reprocessed back to its raw material.
Plastics are the ideal material of choice in packaging applications because of their performance, cost-effectiveness, and durability. To be useful, packaging must safely protect and deliver a product from the manufacturer to the consumer. Plastic packaging helps protect and preserve goods while reducing weight in transportation, which saves fuel and reduces greenhouse gas emissions. Every industry relies on product packaging. Plastics encourage cutting edge design to help deliver innovative packaging solutions that use less material, protect fragile goods, and help cut down on transportation costs - all important elements of sustainability. Plastic packaging is designed to be durable and tough; they are highly versatile and useful materials. Plastic packaging can have numerous benefits - they just need to be reused and recycled as much as possible. Everyone has a role to play in preventing plastic pollution, not just waste management providers. We have a responsibility to the environment, but also to the economy and society.
